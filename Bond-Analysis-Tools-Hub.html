How to Choose

1. Analysis Flowchart: Best for quick decisions based on critical gates. Stops analysis as soon as a dealbreaker is found.

2. Valuation Screener: best for detailed calculated valuation. Great for domestic bonds with clear YTM benchmarks.

3. Bond Screener: Best for analyzing international bonds. Accounts for currency depreciation and home inflation.

4. Complete Evaluation: Institutional-grade analysis with 7 phases covering real returns, interest rate risk, and credit risk.

5. Screener Pro: Best for relative scoring. Ranks multiple bonds comparatively using a points-based system.
